This file contains all the key_bindings that you can use within the game city of Division. Think of this as a simplified version of a documentation.

In the Menus:
- Left or up arrow: Go back one menu item
- Right or down arrow: Move forward one menu item
- End: Go to the bottom menu item
- Home: Go to the top menu item
- Page up or down: Manage the volume of the music if it is in a menu.
- Control + F: Search for a menu item
- Characters: Jump to menu items that start with those characters

In the Game:
- Arrow keys: Move in that respective direction
- Page up / down: climb up or down if possible.
- Shift + arrow keys: Use the radar in that respective direction. To turn off the radar, press the shift + same arrow key again.
- Q: Rotate 45 degrees to the left
- E: Rotate 45 degrees to the right
- Shift + S: turn 180 degrees.
- S: Rotate 45 degrees upward
- X: Rotate 45 degrees downward
- F: Check your facing
- H: Check your health
- C: Check your coordinates
- B: Check your current location
- L: Display a list of all locations in the game with the ability to track them
- shift + c: view all the corpses around you in 30 tiles range with the ability to track them.
- N: Get instructions on how to reach your selected location or corpse
- Shift + N: Stop tracking the location you're tracking
- Home: increase master volume
- End: decrease master volume
- Tab: Open the weapon menu. Further instructions are provited later in the document.
- Left or right control: Fire your current weapon
- A: Check the remaining bullets in your current weapon if possible
- R: Reload your weapon if possible.
- D: Use the scope on your weapon if possible. Further instructions are provited later in the document.
- g or shift + g: use a skill. if it isn't a permanent skill such as the sprint skill.
- Z: Search an object if you are standing right in front of it. Pressing this key while examining will stop the action.
- V: Use the infra_redscanner that plays beeps at the locations where players are within 40 tiles of you
- shift V: Anounce how many players there were scanned during the last scan.
- W: Look 30 meters ahead.
- Shift + w: switch between the  differend scanner modes
- Space: Jump
- Alt + left_and_right_arrow keys: Navigate through your inventory
- Alt + G: Give an item to someone
- Alt + I: Get information about the currently focussed item
- alt + s: store the selected item in a locker if you are standing infront of it.
- Enter: Use the selected item from your inventory
- accent grave: Draw A weapon from your holster if there is one inside of it.
- Shift + accent grave: get information about the weapon that is in your holster
- Slash: Chat
- y: team chat
- Comma and period: Navigate through the buffer items
- Square brackets: Navigate through the buffers
- Backslash: Reply to your last received private message
- backspace: When focused on  a message a player has sent, this will open A menu with actions for this particular player.
- shift + backspace: when in the chat buffer, You can reply to the currently focused message
- I: Get information about your account, such as your level, survivel_time etcetera.
- F1: Ping your connection
- F2: Open the player menu where you can send a message to someone or view their profile
- F3: Open the settings menu, also accessible from the main menu
- F4: Open the server menu
- F5: Mute or unmute a buffer

Within the Weapon Menu:
- Up and Down Arrow_keys: Navigate through the weapon_categories, such as melee, pistols, shotguns, and assault_rifles
- Left and Right Arrows: Browse through the weapons in the selected category
- I: Receive some information about the currently selected weapon
- Space: check how many you have of that weapon.
- A: Check the  remaining ammunition for the currently selected weapon
- Enter: Equip the selected weapon
- Shift + Enter: Put the selected weapon in your sight_holster

While using the Scope:
- Up and Down Arrows: zoom in or out within the scope's view
- Left and Right Arrows: Switch the viewing_point between yourself and the scope
- Shift + D: Check the coordinates the scope is aimed to.

In the lobby
- enter: opens the lobby menu.
- most of the keys are available in the lobby, such as the buffers and the server menus such as the server menu on the f4 key.

Within the settings_menu:
- Tab: Move forward one item.
- Shift + tab: Move backward one item.
- space: Check or uncheck A checkbox
- Enter: Activate A item.
