With some weapons you can use the scope function. You can use this by clicking D when you have drawn this weapon.
The scope gives you access to zoom in and hear the sounds at the position you are scoped at.
the following keys are available when using the scope.
- up and down arrows; zoom in or out with the scope
- left and right arrows: change view between the scope and the view of your player.
- D: stops using the scope
- Shift + D: Checks the coordinates your scope is aimed at.
