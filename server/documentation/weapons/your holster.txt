Because the weapon menu does not allow you to quickly switch weapons, you can use your holster to solve this problem.
You take the weapon from your holster by clicking on the accent grave key.
To put a weapon in your holster, click shift enter on it in the weapon menu.
You check which weapon is in your holster by clicking shift + accent grave.
To put the weapon you're holding into your holster, you press alt accent grave.
It is important to know that you can only put melee weapons, pistols and sub_machine_guns in your holster. You can holster other types of weapons by activating a skill called holster augmentation. If you upgrade this skill you can also holster shotguns, assault_rifles and sniper_rifles.
